\begin{block}{1. Background \& Motivation}
    \textbf{The Problem:} Bike Sharing Systems (BSS) face two critical challenges:
    
    \vspace{0.3cm}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{Demand-Supply Imbalance:} Stations become empty or full, causing user dissatisfaction
        \item \textbf{Broken Bikes:} Barcelona: 12\% severe + 55\% light damage; NYC: 2\% need daily repairs
    \end{itemize}
    
    \vspace{0.3cm}
    \textbf{Research Gap:} No existing study jointly optimizes:
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item Vehicle-based repositioning (usable \& broken bikes)
        \item \textcolor{AlertRed}{\textbf{Labor-based on-site repairs}}
    \end{enumerate}
